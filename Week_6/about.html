About Google

  • Founded on September 4,1998,by Larry Page and Sergey Brin.
  • They made google while they were studying on Stanford University.
  • Google LLC is a subsidiary of Alphabet Inc.
  • Google is widely recognized for its dominance in online advertising, search engine technology, and AI.
  • known for revolutionizing the tech industry with innovative products like Google Search, Gmail, Youtube, and Android.
  • Referred to as "the most powerful company in the world" by the BBC.

Key Products and Services:

  • Google Search: The largest search engine in the world.
  • Gmail: Popular email service with billions of users.
  • Google Maps: A leading mapping and navigation application.
  • Google Cloud: Cloud computing services for businesses and developers.
  • YouTube: The largest online video platform.
  • Google Drive: Cloud storage services offering free along with paid plans.
  • Google Assistant: AI-based virtual assistant for voice and smart home controls.

Google's Major Acquisition:

  • YouTube: Acquired in 2006 for 1.65 billion dollar.
  • Android: Acquired in 2005, one of the most widely used OS.
  • DoubleClick: Acquired in 2008, to enhance the advertising services.
  • DeepMind: Acquired in 2015 for Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning.

Founders and Leaders:

Larry Page: Co-founder of Google, former CEO of Google.

Sergey Brin: Co-founder of Google, responsible for various Google Innovation.

Sundar Pichai: Current CEO of Google.

Discontinued Google Products

  • Google Stadia: Cloud gaming service discontinued in 2023.
  • Google Glass: Smart glasses discontinued due to low adoption.
  • Google Plus: Social network discontinued in 2019.
  • Google Play Music: Music streaming service replaced by YouTube Music.
